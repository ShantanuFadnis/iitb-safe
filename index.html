Vision for a Smart Classroom for future

What is SAFE?

Smart Authenticated Fast Exams

With SAFE, conducting objective exams is paper-free and cheating-free.

It eliminates the tedious process of manual evaluation and eases the conduct of surveys or polls.

SAFE allows students to mark attendance and take quiz in classroom using their own smartphones.

Some Potential

Use Cases

Quick, Proxy free Attendance:

With SAFE you get an amazing feature of quick, proxy-free attendance marking.

Continuous assessment through short quizzes:

You can conduct short quizzes, as easy as asking a verbal question in class. These help in instant feedback to the student as well as teacher.

Online correction of regular subjective exams:

Student answers subjective question on paper, scans and uploads. Teacher or teaching assistants can manage all correction online: no manual touching of student’s paper during correction. SAFE supports answer rubrics: reuse feedback from previous student’s answered corrections.

Easy, paper-free objective exams:

Get rid of the hassle of printing and manual evaluation. With SAFE, conducting objective exams is paper-free and cheating-free.

Check mental presence:

Are your students mentally present? Have they grasped what you just taught? With a short SAFE-quiz in the class, get instant feedback; you don’t need sophisticated hardware clicker devices!

Classroom mode:

The teacher can require students to keep their phones in “classroom mode” of the SAFE app, during which the phone can be used only for SAFE attendance or quiz but not for other apps.

Surveys and polls:

SAFE eases the conduct of surveys or polls,with configurable anonymity for those answering.
